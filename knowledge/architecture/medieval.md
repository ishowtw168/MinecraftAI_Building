# Medieval Architecture

Medieval architecture is one of the most common building styles in Minecraft. It emphasizes practicality, strong silhouettes, and natural materials, making it suitable for villages, castles, kingdoms, RPG maps, and fantasy worlds.

---

# Characteristics

- Functional design
- Thick stone foundations
- Timber-framed walls
- Steep roofs
- Simple decorative details
- Defensive structures

---

# Design Principles

- Build from large shapes before adding details.
- Use roof variation to create visual interest.
- Keep a consistent material palette.
- Add depth with beams, windows, and balconies.
- Avoid flat walls.
