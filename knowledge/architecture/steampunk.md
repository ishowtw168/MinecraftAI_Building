# Steampunk Architecture

Steampunk architecture combines Victorian-era design with industrial machinery. It features exposed mechanical elements, metal structures, and large-scale engineering, making it ideal for factories, airships, industrial cities, and themed attractions.

---

# Characteristics

- Industrial appearance
- Exposed machinery
- Large metal structures
- Pipes and gears
- Tall smokestacks
- Mechanical details

# Design Principles

- Expose structural elements.
- Combine metal with wood.
- Create strong vertical silhouettes.
- Use pipes and machinery as decoration.
- Add weathering for an aged industrial look.
