# Fantasy Architecture

## Overview

Fantasy architecture is not limited by real-world engineering.
Its purpose is to inspire wonder, mystery, and imagination while remaining believable within Minecraft.

Fantasy builds should create memorable silhouettes, strong visual identities, and immersive environments.

---

# Core Principles

- Prioritize imagination over realism.
- Design recognizable silhouettes.
- Blend structures with the surrounding environment.
- Use landmarks to guide exploration.
- Balance beauty and functionality.

# Design Guidelines

## Roofs

- Avoid flat roofs.
- Mix roof heights.
- Use overhangs for depth.

---

## Towers

- Vary tower heights.
- Add observation decks.
- Avoid identical shapes.

---

## Entrances

- Make entrances visually important.
- Use lighting to attract attention.

---

## Color Palette

Fantasy builds often use:

- Green
- White
- Gold
- Purple
- Dark Gray

Choose one dominant color and two supporting colors.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Buildings are too symmetrical.
- Every building has the same height.
- No focal point.
- Too many block types.
- Empty walls without depth.

---

# AI Design Checklist

Before finishing a fantasy design, ask:

- Does the build have a clear landmark?
- Does the silhouette look interesting?
- Is the palette consistent?
- Does it fit the surrounding environment?
- Would players remember this place?
