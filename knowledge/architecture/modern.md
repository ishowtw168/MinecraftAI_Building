# Modern Architecture

Modern architecture focuses on simplicity, functionality, and clean geometry. It emphasizes open spaces, minimal decoration, and the use of contemporary materials, making it suitable for cities, luxury homes, offices, museums, and modern districts.

---

# Characteristics

- Clean geometric shapes
- Minimal decoration
- Large glass surfaces
- Open floor plans
- Functional layouts
- Strong horizontal and vertical lines

# Design Principles

- Prioritize simple forms over excessive detail.
- Keep the material palette consistent.
- Maximize natural lighting with large windows.
- Balance solid walls with open spaces.
- Use landscaping to soften clean architectural lines.
