# Landmarks

## Overview

A landmark is a visually distinctive element that helps players recognize, remember, and navigate a Minecraft world.

Good landmarks improve exploration and make worlds feel memorable.

---

# Why Landmarks Matter

Landmarks help players:

- Navigate naturally.
- Remember important locations.
- Feel oriented.
- Create memorable experiences.

---

# Characteristics of Good Landmarks

- Visible from a distance.
- Unique silhouette.
- Strong contrast with surroundings.
- Easy to describe.
- Connected to the world's story.

---

# Types of Landmarks

## Natural

- Giant tree
- Waterfall
- Volcano
- Mountain
- Floating island

---

## Architectural

- Castle
- Tower
- Temple
- Statue
- Cathedral

---

## Fantasy

- World Tree
- Dragon Skeleton
- Floating Crystal
- Ancient Portal

---

# Placement Guidelines

Avoid placing multiple major landmarks too close together.

Give each landmark visual space.

Ensure players can discover them naturally.

---

# Common Mistakes

- Every structure is equally tall.
- No visual focus.
- Landmarks hidden behind terrain.
- Repeating identical landmarks.

---

# AI Design Checklist

□ Can players recognize it instantly?

□ Can players see it from multiple angles?

□ Does it support navigation?

□ Is it unique within the world?
