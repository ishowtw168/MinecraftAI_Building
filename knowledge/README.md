# Minecraft AI Studio Knowledge Base

## Purpose

The Knowledge Base stores reusable design knowledge shared by all Studio members.

Instead of embedding knowledge inside individual AI roles, it is organized into reusable documents.

This makes the Studio easier to maintain, expand, and improve over time.

---

## Categories

### Architecture

Building styles and architectural principles.

### Theme Parks

Theme park planning, guest flow, attraction design, and immersion.

### Worldbuilding

Creating believable Minecraft worlds.

### Block Palettes

Recommended block combinations and color palettes.

### Biomes

Biome characteristics and design ideas.

### Storytelling

Narrative design, lore creation, and quests.

### Technical

Minecraft mechanics, commands, datapacks, and optimization.

---

## Design Philosophy

Knowledge should be:

- Reusable
- Well documented
- Easy to understand
- Independent of specific AI roles
