# Deserts

Deserts create harsh and open environments that contrast with lush biomes. Their simple terrain and limited vegetation make landmarks and architecture stand out, making them ideal for ancient civilizations, ruins, and survival challenges.

---

# Characteristics

- Dry climate
- Wide open spaces
- Sparse vegetation
- Rolling dunes
- Rocky plateaus
- High visibility

---

# Terrain Features

## Sand Dunes

- Gentle slopes
- Wind-shaped patterns
- Layered sand

## Rocky Areas

- Sandstone cliffs
- Canyons
- Stone outcrops

## Oasis

- Small lakes
- Palm trees
- Lush vegetation
- Settlements

---

# Design Principles

- Avoid perfectly flat terrain.
- Use dunes to create depth.
- Place landmarks far apart to encourage exploration.
- Build settlements around water sources.
- Contrast open landscapes with detailed structures.
