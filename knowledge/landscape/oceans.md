# Oceans

Oceans create vast open spaces, separate continents, and encourage long-distance exploration. They provide contrast to land environments and support coastal settlements, islands, and underwater locations.

---

# Characteristics

- Large open water
- Natural world boundaries
- Long-distance travel routes
- Coastal environments
- Underwater ecosystems
- Island formation

---

# Terrain Features

## Open Ocean

## Islands

- Small islets
- Large islands

- Tropical islands

---

# Design Principles

- Break up large oceans with islands.
- Create varied coastlines instead of straight edges.
- Design natural harbors for ports.
- Use underwater features to encourage exploration.
- Place major landmarks where they are visible from the sea.
