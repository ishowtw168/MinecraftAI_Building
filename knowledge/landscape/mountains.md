# Mountains

Mountains create natural landmarks, define world boundaries, and add vertical exploration. They are one of the strongest visual elements in Minecraft world design.

---

# Characteristics

- High elevation
- Strong silhouettes
- Natural barriers
- Layered terrain
- Distinct landmarks
- Long-distance visibility

---

# Terrain Features

## Peaks

- Sharp summits
- Snow-covered tops
- Rocky cliffs

## Slopes

- Mixed gradients
- Exposed stone
- Scattered vegetation

## Valleys

- Rivers
- Forests
- Villages
- Roads

---

# Common Blocks

## Rock

- Cobblestone
- Deepslate

## Vegetation

- Spruce Trees
- Pine Trees
- Grass
- Ferns
- Moss

## Snow Zone

- Snow Blocks
- Powder Snow
- Ice
- Packed Ice

---

# Design Principles

- Build large landforms before adding details.
- Avoid perfectly symmetrical peaks.
- Create multiple elevation levels.
- Mix cliffs with gentler slopes.
- Use vegetation to transition between terrain types.

---
