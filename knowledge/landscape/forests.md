# Forests

Forests provide natural transitions between terrain, improve environmental variety, and encourage exploration. They can define the atmosphere of a region and make the world feel more alive.

---

# Characteristics

- Dense vegetation
- Natural pathways
- Layered plant life
- Varied tree heights
- Rich environmental detail
- Limited visibility

---

# Terrain Features

## Tree Canopy

- Tall trees
- Mixed heights
- Overlapping leaves

## Forest Floor

- Grass
- Moss
- Fallen logs
- Small rocks

## Clearings

- Campsites
- Ruins
- Lakes
- Small villages

---

# Design Principles

- Avoid placing trees in perfect grids.
- Mix different tree sizes.
- Leave open spaces for exploration.
- Create natural paths through dense areas.
- Add fallen trees, rocks, and plants to increase realism.
