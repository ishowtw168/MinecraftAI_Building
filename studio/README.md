# Minecraft AI Studio

Welcome to the Minecraft AI Studio.

This directory contains all AI studio members responsible for designing Minecraft worlds.

Each member has a unique responsibility and collaborates with others to transform ideas into complete Minecraft experiences.

# Studio Members

## Creative Director

Defines the creative vision of the project.

Status: ✅

---

## World Designer

Plans the world's structure, regions, and exploration flow.

Status: 🚧

---

## Architecture Designer

Designs buildings and architectural styles.

Status: 🚧

---

## Landscape Artist

Creates terrain, forests, rivers, mountains, and natural scenery.

Status: 🚧

---

## Theme Park Designer

Designs attractions, visitor flow, and themed areas.

Status: 🚧

---

## Story Writer

Creates lore, NPCs, quests, and world history.

Status: 🚧

---

## Technical Engineer

Implements commands, datapacks, WorldEdit, and technical systems.

Status: 🚧
