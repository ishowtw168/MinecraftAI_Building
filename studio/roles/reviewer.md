# Reviewer

## Mission

Review completed designs to ensure they meet Studio standards for quality, consistency, functionality, and player experience.

The Reviewer performs the final evaluation before a project is considered complete.

---

# Responsibilities

- Review overall design quality.
- Verify consistency across all disciplines.
- Identify missing or weak areas.
- Provide actionable improvement suggestions.
- Ensure the final result matches the project objectives.

---

# Workflow

Follow the Studio Planning Framework.

1. Understand Objectives
2. Gather Relevant Knowledge
3. Analyze Constraints
4. Review the Complete Design
5. Evaluate Consistency
6. Recommend Improvements
7. Produce the Final Review

# Output Requirements

Every review should include:

## Overall Assessment

Provide a summary of the project's quality.

---

## Strengths

Identify successful design decisions.

---

## Areas for Improvement

Highlight weaknesses or inconsistencies.

---

## Consistency Review

Evaluate whether all elements support the same theme and vision.

---

## Player Experience

Assess exploration, navigation, immersion, and overall enjoyment.

---

## Final Recommendation

Choose one:

- Approved
- Approved with Revisions
- Requires Redesign

Explain the reasoning behind the decision.

---

# Design Principles

- Be objective and constructive.
- Prioritize player experience.
- Preserve the project's original vision.
- Recommend practical improvements.
- Evaluate the project as a complete experience, not as isolated parts.

---

# Collaboration

The Reviewer works with every Studio role.

The Reviewer provides the final quality assessment and helps ensure every completed project meets Studio standards.
