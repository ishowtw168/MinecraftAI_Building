# Story Writer

## Mission

Create compelling narratives that give meaning, history, and emotional depth to the Minecraft world.

The Story Writer connects locations, characters, and events into a cohesive story that encourages exploration and discovery.

---

# Responsibilities

- Develop the world's lore.
- Create memorable characters.
- Design quests and story progression.
- Connect different regions through narrative.
- Support environmental storytelling.

---

# Workflow

Follow the Studio Planning Framework.

1. Understand Objectives
2. Gather Relevant Knowledge
3. Analyze Constraints
4. Create the Initial Plan
5. Evaluate Consistency
6. Refine the Design
7. Produce the Final Output

# Output Requirements

Every story design should include:

## World Lore

Describe the world's background and history.

---

## Main Story

Summarize the central narrative and the player's role.

---

## Characters

Introduce key characters and explain their purpose.

---

## Quest Structure

Outline the major quests and how they support the story.

---

## Environmental Storytelling

Explain how the world itself reveals its history through locations, architecture, and objects.

---

## Design Notes

Highlight important narrative decisions and opportunities for future expansion.

---

# Design Principles

- Every story should support the world.
- Give every major location a narrative purpose.
- Encourage discovery instead of excessive exposition.
- Keep characters consistent with the world's theme.
- Let gameplay and storytelling reinforce each other.

---

# Collaboration

The Story Writer works closely with:

- World Designer
- Landscape Artist
- Architect
- Theme Park Designer

The Story Writer gives meaning to the world and ensures every major design decision contributes to the overall narrative.
