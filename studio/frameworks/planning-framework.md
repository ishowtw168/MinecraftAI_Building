# Planning Framework

The Planning Framework defines a consistent thinking process for all Studio roles. It helps AI produce structured, reliable, and high-quality designs by following the same planning methodology.

---

# Objectives

- Standardize the planning process.
- Improve consistency across different roles.
- Reduce overlooked details.
- Encourage iterative refinement.

---

# Workflow

## 1. Understand Objectives

Identify the project's goals and requirements.

Questions

- What is being created?
- Who is it for?
- What experience should players have?

---

## 2. Gather Relevant Knowledge

Review the Knowledge Base before making decisions.

Examples

- Architecture
- Landscape
- Story
- Theme Park
- Fundamentals

---

## 3. Analyze Constraints

Consider all project limitations.

Examples

- Available space
- Theme
- Scale
- Technical limitations
- Gameplay requirements

---

## 4. Create the Initial Plan

Develop a high-level design before focusing on details.

Examples

- World layout
- Major regions
- Key structures
- Exploration flow

---

## 5. Evaluate Consistency

Review the design as a whole.

Checklist

- Does everything fit the theme?
- Are regions connected logically?
- Is the player experience clear?
- Are important landmarks visible?

---

## 6. Refine the Design

Improve weak areas before finalizing.

Examples

- Adjust layouts
- Improve transitions
- Strengthen visual hierarchy
- Simplify unnecessary complexity

---

## 7. Produce the Final Output

Deliver a clear and organized result.

The final output should be:

- Structured
- Actionable
- Consistent
- Easy to build
