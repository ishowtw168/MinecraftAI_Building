# Studio Workflow

## Overview

Minecraft AI Studio follows a structured design process.

Each studio member contributes within their area of expertise and passes the project to the next member.

The goal is to combine creativity, design, storytelling, and technical implementation into one coherent workflow.

---

# Workflow

```
User Idea
    │
    ▼
Creative Director
    │
    ▼
World Designer
    │
    ▼
Architecture Designer
    │
    ▼
Landscape Artist
    │
    ▼
Theme Park Designer
    │
    ▼
Story Writer
    │
    ▼
Technical Engineer
```

---

# Responsibilities

## Creative Director

Defines the project's vision.

Output:

- Theme
- Mood
- Core experience

---

## World Designer

Plans the world's layout.

Output:

- Regions
- Progression
- Transportation

---

## Architecture Designer

Designs buildings.

Output:

- Styles
- Block palettes
- Landmark structures

---

## Landscape Artist

Shapes the environment.

Output:

- Terrain
- Vegetation
- Natural landmarks

---

## Theme Park Designer

Creates attractions and guest experience.

Output:

- Attractions
- Guest flow
- Supporting facilities

---

## Story Writer

Creates lore and narrative.

Output:

- History
- Characters
- Quests

---

## Technical Engineer

Plans implementation.

Output:

- Commands
- Datapacks
- Optimization
- Technical recommendations

---

# Design Rules

Each member should:

- Read previous outputs.
- Respect previous decisions.
- Explain important changes.
- Avoid overwriting another member's work without justification.

---

# Final Deliverables

A completed project should include:

- Creative Vision
- World Plan
- Architecture Plan
- Landscape Plan
- Theme Park Plan
- Story Plan
- Technical Plan
