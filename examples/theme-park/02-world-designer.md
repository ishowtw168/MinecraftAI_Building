# World Designer Output

## Project

Sky Isles Theme Park

Version: 1.0

Based on:
- Project Brief
- Creative Director Output

---

# World Layout Overview

The park consists of two major floating islands connected by a central transportation hub.

The layout encourages players to choose their own path while maintaining clear visual orientation.

```
             Fantasy Kingdom
                    ▲
                    │
              Sky Bridge
                    │
            Central Hub
                    │
              Sky Bridge
                    │
                    ▼
             Ancient Desert
```

---

# Island Placement

## Fantasy Kingdom

Location:
North of the Central Hub.

Purpose:
The welcoming island where players begin their adventure.

Primary Landmark:
Grand Castle

Secondary Landmarks:
- Giant Tree
- Wizard Tower

Terrain:
Rolling hills, rivers, forests, and cliffs.

---

## Ancient Desert

Location:
South of the Central Hub.

Purpose:
A mysterious destination focused on exploration.

Primary Landmark:
Great Pyramid

Secondary Landmarks:
- Oasis
- Desert Temple
- Giant Statue

Terrain:
Sand dunes, rocky plateaus, canyons.

---

# Central Hub

Purpose:

The transition point between islands.

Features:

- Information Center
- Portal Plaza
- Food Court
- Observation Tower

The hub should never become the main attraction.

Its role is orientation and transportation.

---

# Transportation

Players may travel using:

- Walking
- Decorative Sky Bridges
- Minecart Railway
- Elytra Launch Tower (late-game)

Transportation should feel like part of the experience rather than a loading screen.

---

# Sightlines

Players should immediately see:

From the Hub:

- Fantasy Castle
- Great Pyramid

From Fantasy Kingdom:

- Wizard Tower
- Floating Islands
- Distant Pyramid

From Ancient Desert:

- Oasis
- Castle silhouette

This reinforces exploration and orientation.

---

# Expansion Plan

Reserve space for future islands.

Possible additions:

- Pirate Island
- Steampunk City
- Snow Kingdom
- Underwater Realm

Expansion should not require redesigning the original layout.

---

# World Design Principles

- Strong landmark visibility.
- Logical navigation.
- Smooth transitions.
- Distinct island identities.
- Balanced travel time.

---

# Recommendations for Architecture Designer

Focus on:

- Building scale.
- Architectural language.
- Material palettes.
- Entrance design.
- Skyline composition.

Avoid changing island locations or overall layout.
