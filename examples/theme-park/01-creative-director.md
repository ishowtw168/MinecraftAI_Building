# Creative Director Output

## Project

Sky Isles Theme Park

Version: 1.0

---

# Executive Summary

Sky Isles Theme Park is a fantasy-themed Minecraft destination built across two floating islands.

The park encourages exploration through strong visual landmarks, immersive environments, and distinct themed experiences. Every island should immediately communicate its identity while inviting players to discover what lies beyond.

The overall feeling should be magical, adventurous, and rewarding.

---

# Design Vision

The park should feel like a living world rather than a collection of rides.

Players should naturally move between attractions through curiosity instead of explicit directions.

Exploration should always reveal something new.

---

# Core Experience

Players should experience:

- Wonder
- Discovery
- Adventure
- Relaxation between major attractions
- Excitement at key landmarks

Every journey should alternate between calm moments and exciting discoveries.

---

# Design Pillars

## Exploration First

Reward curiosity.

Avoid long empty paths.

---

## Every Island Has an Identity

No island should feel interchangeable.

Each must have its own:

- Architecture
- Terrain
- Atmosphere
- Music inspiration
- Color palette

---

## Strong Visual Storytelling

Players should understand an area simply by looking at it.

Large landmarks should communicate the island's purpose.

---

## Memorable Skyline

The park skyline should be recognizable from almost anywhere.

Each island should contribute a unique silhouette.

# Risks

- Islands becoming visually similar.
- Attractions overwhelming the landscape.
- Excessive decoration reducing readability.
- Weak transitions between themed areas.

---

# Recommendations for the World Designer

The World Designer should focus on:

- Overall island placement.
- Travel routes.
- Sightlines.
- Landmark visibility.
- Scale relationships.

Do not begin detailed architecture yet.
