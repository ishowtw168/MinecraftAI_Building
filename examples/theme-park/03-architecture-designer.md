# Architecture Designer Output

## Project

Sky Isles Theme Park

Version: 1.0

Based on:
- Project Brief
- Creative Director Output
- World Designer Output

---

# Objective

Develop a consistent architectural language for each island.

Architecture should reinforce the identity established by previous Studio members while remaining practical for Minecraft construction.

---

# Fantasy Kingdom

## Architectural Style

High Fantasy

Inspired by:

- Medieval European castles
- Elven architecture
- Fairy tale villages

---

## Building Materials

Primary

- Stone Bricks
- Smooth Stone
- White Concrete
- Quartz

Secondary

- Spruce Wood
- Dark Oak
- Mossy Stone Bricks

Decoration

- Lanterns
- Banners
- Leaves
- Flowers

---

## Roof Design

- Tall roofs
- Multiple roof layers
- Blue or dark cyan accents
- Decorative chimneys
- Small towers

---

## Windows

- Tall vertical windows
- Arched tops
- Warm lighting

---

## Landmark

Grand Castle

Requirements:

- Highest structure on the island.
- Visible from every major path.
- Large central courtyard.
- Four surrounding towers.
- Distinct silhouette.

---

## Supporting Buildings

- Blacksmith
- Inn
- Bakery
- Wizard Tower
- Market

Buildings should vary in height and footprint to avoid repetition.

---

# Ancient Desert

## Architectural Style

Ancient Civilization

Inspired by:

- Egyptian monuments
- Desert temples
- Massive stone structures

---

## Building Materials

Primary

- Smooth Sandstone
- Cut Sandstone
- Chiseled Sandstone

Secondary

- Terracotta
- Calcite
- Gold Blocks (limited)

Decoration

- Palm Trees
- Statues
- Obelisks
- Water Features

---

## Roof Design

Mostly flat roofs.

Monumental buildings should emphasize mass rather than height.

---

## Landmark

Great Pyramid

Requirements:

- Dominates the skyline.
- Visible from the Central Hub.
- Multiple entrances.
- Hidden interior spaces.

---

## Supporting Buildings

- Oasis Village
- Archaeology Camp
- Temple Ruins
- Marketplace

---

# Shared Design Rules

Every building should:

- Be recognizable from a distance.
- Have a clear entrance.
- Use a limited material palette.
- Match the island's identity.
- Avoid unnecessary complexity.

---

# Scale Guide

Small Building

10–15 blocks wide.

Medium Building

20–35 blocks wide.

Large Building

40–60 blocks wide.

Landmarks

80+ blocks.

---

# Recommendations for Landscape Artist

The landscape should enhance architecture rather than compete with it.

Terrain should naturally frame important structures and preserve key sightlines established by the World Designer.
