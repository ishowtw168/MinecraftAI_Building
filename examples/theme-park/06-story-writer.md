# Objective

Create a narrative that connects every island into one believable world.

The story should enrich exploration without forcing players to read large amounts of text.

---

# World Story

Long ago, Sky Isles was a single floating continent sustained by a mysterious source of magic known as the Heart of the Sky.

Over time, the Heart fractured, causing the land to split into separate floating islands.

Each island preserved part of the world's history and developed its own culture.

Today, explorers travel between the islands to uncover their forgotten connections.

---

# Island Stories

## Fantasy Kingdom

The kingdom once protected the Heart of the Sky.

Its castle, villages, and great tree remain symbols of hope.

Although peaceful, traces of ancient magic can still be found throughout the forests.

---

## Ancient Desert

The desert was once a thriving civilization devoted to knowledge and astronomy.

Its people attempted to harness the Heart's power, but their experiments ended in disaster.

Only ruins, temples, and buried treasures remain.

---

# Player Role

The player is an explorer.

Rather than saving the world, the player's purpose is to rediscover its history through exploration.

The story unfolds naturally by visiting attractions and observing the environment.

---

# Environmental Storytelling

The world should reveal its history through details such as:

- Broken statues
- Ancient murals
- Abandoned camps
- Hidden libraries
- Weathered pathways

Players should be rewarded for paying attention.

---

# Attraction Integration

Every major attraction should contribute to the story.

Fantasy Coaster

Players journey through the kingdom's golden age before entering forgotten underground ruins.

Great Pyramid Adventure

Players uncover evidence that the ancient civilization once studied the same magical power that sustained Fantasy Kingdom.

---

# Optional Collectibles

Players may discover:

- Ancient journals
- Lost maps
- Magic crystals
- Historical artifacts

Collectibles deepen the story without being required.

---

# Tone

The world should feel:

- Hopeful
- Mysterious
- Adventurous
- Timeless

Humor may appear occasionally, but should never undermine the atmosphere.

---

# Recommendations for Technical Engineer

Implement gameplay systems that support exploration.

Examples include:

- Collectible tracking
- Achievement system
- Ride triggers
- Environmental sound effects
- Hidden secrets
