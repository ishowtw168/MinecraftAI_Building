# Example Project

## Ancient Egypt Theme Island

This document demonstrates how every Studio role collaborates to transform a user request into a complete Minecraft project.

---

# User Request

Design an Ancient Egypt themed island for a Minecraft amusement park.

Requirements

- Ancient Egyptian theme
- Escape room experience
- Roller coaster attraction
- Exploration-focused
- Strong environmental storytelling

---

# Stage 1 — World Designer

## Theme

Ancient Egypt

## World Structure

- Harbor
- Desert Entrance
- Ancient Village
- Pyramid Complex
- Royal Tomb
- Roller Coaster Area

## Landmarks

- Great Pyramid
- Giant Sphinx
- Obelisk Plaza

## Exploration Flow

Harbor
↓

Village
↓

Temple

↓

Pyramid

↓

Royal Tomb

↓

Roller Coaster Exit

---

# Handoff

The world is centered around an ancient kingdom buried beneath the desert.

Players gradually uncover the civilization as they explore deeper into the island.

---

# Stage 5 — Story Writer

## World Lore

The Pharaoh sealed an ancient power beneath the pyramid.

Centuries later, explorers unknowingly awaken it.

## Characters

- Pharaoh
- High Priest
- Explorer

## Main Quest

Escape the pyramid before the curse spreads.

---

# Handoff

Environmental clues should tell the story instead of long dialogue.

# Stage 7 — Reviewer

## Strengths

- Strong visual hierarchy.
- Clear exploration path.
- Consistent architecture.
- Story supports gameplay.

## Improvements

- Expand the oasis.
- Add more environmental storytelling.
- Increase transition details between village and pyramid.
